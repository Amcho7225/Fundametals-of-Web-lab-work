Final Fantasy 7 Rebirth

About

Final Fantasy 7 Rebirth is a remake of the classic RPG game Final Fantasy 7. It features stunning graphics, revamped gameplay, and an expanded storyline.

Set in the dystopian city of Midgar, players control Cloud Strife, a former SOLDIER turned mercenary, as he joins forces with a group of rebels known as AVALANCHE to stop the corrupt Shinra Electric Power Company from exploiting the planet's resources.

The game combines elements of fantasy, science fiction, and environmentalism, exploring themes of identity, friendship, and the consequences of unchecked capitalism.

Characters

Cloud Strife

An enigmatic former SOLDIER turned mercenary, Cloud Strife wields his iconic Buster Sword with unparalleled skill and determination. Amidst his mysterious past and inner turmoil, he rises as a reluctant hero, leading his allies in the battle against evil.

Aerith Gainsborough

A gentle yet resilient flower seller with a mysterious connection to the planet, Aerith Gainsborough possesses a kind heart and unwavering courage. As a beacon of hope amidst chaos, she guides her friends with wisdom and grace, determined to thwart the plans of those who seek to harm the world.

Tifa Lockhart

A resilient and compassionate martial artist, Tifa Lockhart stands as a pillar of strength among her companions. With her unwavering determination and formidable combat skills, she fights fiercely to protect her friends and the planet from the forces of darkness.

Barret Wallace

A passionate and outspoken leader, Barret Wallace commands respect with his imposing presence and powerful gun arm. Fueled by a deep sense of justice and a desire to protect the planet, he fights tirelessly alongside his allies in the struggle against oppression.
