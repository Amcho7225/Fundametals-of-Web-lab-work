About Us

Vault-Tec Industries, a prominent pre-war corporation, specialized in constructing underground Vaults, vital shelters designed to protect against nuclear fallout. Founded in the mid-21st century, Vault-Tec played a pivotal role in government preparations for nuclear war. Renowned for its innovative technology and visionary engineering, Vault-Tec's mission extended beyond construction to encompass comprehensive community development and resource management. With a commitment to safeguarding humanity's future, Vault-Tec stood as a beacon of hope amidst global uncertainty, dedicated to preserving life and nurturing resilient societies in the face of impending catastrophe.
